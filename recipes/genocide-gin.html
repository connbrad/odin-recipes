Odin's Conflagration Corn

Description:

Here you will find a recipe for Odin's favorite spicy corn. He likes his corn as spicy as his conflagrations!

Ingredients:

  • 2 (15 ounce) cans corn, drained
  • 1 (4 ounce) can diced pickled jalapeno or (4 ounce) can diced green chilies, drained
  • 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
  • 1‚ÅÑ2 cup cheddar cheese, shredded
  • salt and pepper, to taste

Steps:

  1. Combine all ingredients in a bowl, mixing well.
  2. Put mixture in a greased 9x9 casserole dish.
  3. Bake at 350 degrees for 20 minutes until bubbly.
  4. ---OR--- Simply put it in a microwave safe bowl and microwave for 5 minutes until heated through!
